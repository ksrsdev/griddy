My Advice for your SDL3 Journey

Since you are interested in the "craft," I recommend you stop trying to enforce a resolution. Tiling window managers are the ultimate test for a developer. If your app looks good in a dwl vertical split, it will look good anywhere.

    Remove SDL_SetRenderLogicalPresentation.

    Listen for SDL_EVENT_WINDOW_RESIZED and SDL_EVENT_WINDOW_PIXEL_SIZE_CHANGED.

    Create a simple "UI Scaler" function that takes a coordinate in your "design space" (e.g., 1920x1080) and outputs the real pixel coordinate based on the current SDL_GetWindowSize.
